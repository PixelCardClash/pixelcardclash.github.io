Privacy Policy

Pixel Card Clash highly values the protection of your privacy.

Local Data Storage

The app stores game progress, settings, achievements, and statistics exclusively local on your end device. No personal data is transferred to or stored on external servers. Since all game information is saved locally, this data will be lost if the app is deleted or if you switch devices without a backup.

Local Multiplayer Mode

For local multiplayer matches, the app uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity technology. The connection is established directly and securely between the participating devices via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. No registration is required, and no personal data is transmitted to us at any time.

No Tracking or Third-Party Services

Pixel Card Clash does not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising services from third-party providers. We do not create user profiles and do not track any activities outside of the app.

Apple Services

If system functions provided by Apple are used (e.g., in-app purchases or iCloud backups), the privacy policies of Apple Inc. apply additionally.

Contacting Us

If you contact us via email (e.g., for support inquiries), the information you provide (name, email address, and the content of the message) will be used exclusively to process and answer your request and will be deleted subsequently.

If you have any questions regarding data privacy, please contact us via email.
