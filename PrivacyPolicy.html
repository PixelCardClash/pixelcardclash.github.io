Privacy Policy

Pixel Card Clash places great importance on protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use the app.

The app stores game progress, settings, achievements, and statistics exclusively on your device. No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers.

For local multiplayer matches, Pixel Card Clash uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity technology. Connections are established directly between participating devices and generally do not require registration or the transmission of personal data to us.

Pixel Card Clash does not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services. We do not create user profiles and do not track activities outside the app.

If Apple services are used, such as In-App Purchases or other Apple services, Apple's own privacy policies also apply.

If you contact us via email, the information you provide will be used solely for the purpose of processing your inquiry.

Since all game information is stored locally on your device, it may be lost if the app is deleted.

By using Pixel Card Clash, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions regarding privacy, please contact us by email.
