What makes it different

How the Clash works

Compare card values across six categories and challenge your opponent to a Clash. Whoever has the higher value wins the card. Whoever collects all the cards first wins the game.

Five unique card themes

Home Computers, Video Game Consoles, Arcade Classics, Computer Pioneers and Dead Dotcoms.

Special game rules

Bonus System: Charge up points. Reverse Rounds: Suddenly the lowest value wins! Category Lock: Freeze a category. Comeback Power: Anything is still possible. Pick 1 of 3: Choose one of your top three cards. ...and more

AI or friends

Play against four AI Difficulty Levels OR connect directly with a friend over Wi-Fi. No internet, no server, no account needed — just play!
